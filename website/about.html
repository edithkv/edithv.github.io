MY MAJOR

Hi, my name is Edith and I'm from Jersey City, NJ. I am a sophomore at Cornell University, planning to major in Computer Science (CS) with an external specialization in Information Science, Systems and Technology (ISST).

On campus, I'm a publicity team officer for the Association of Computer Science Undergraduates (ACSU), an economic analyst for Cornell's Design By Biomedical Undergraduate Project Team (DEBUT), and a Business/Logistics subteam member for Cornell Concrete Canoe.
