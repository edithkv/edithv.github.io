COMMUNITY

SUMMER 2019: Girls Who Code Teaching Assistant @Jet.com

This past summer, I worked as a TA at Jet.com for Girls Who Code! It was an amazing experience meeting the newest generation of innovative women curious about technology. Imposter syndrome is a true reality for many women considering tech. When I did this program in 2017, I was empowered by the idea that women and other URM'S are entitled a place in areas of industry they are passionate about. This summer, I wanted to give back. Over the seven weeks, I taught seventeen high school girls Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C++, and databases. I helped maintain relationships with partner company contacts and speakers to facilitate engaging class discussions.


FEB 2019 - PRESENT: Publicity Officer @Association of Computer Science Undergraduates

As part of the publicity team, I help coordinate social outreach events for ACSU. In addition, I promote events including corporate tech talks, peer and faculty mentorship, and career workshops to the entire Cornell student body using social media outlets such as Instagram and Facebook.
